A Minecraft Mod

Back in 2012 I was just starting to experiment with programming. Being a kid, one of my biggest interests was Minecraft. To be more accurate, my obsession was beating other players. I'm not proud, that I did mod a game to gain an unfair advantage, but I can't ignore what I learned, and more importantly, the fire that this experience ignited within me. After that, I realized I needed a menu to toggle these abilities, including som vanity to make it look cool:
I realized my menu was quite obstructive and not that good looking. Here I tried to make it more user friendly and better looking, while adding som features.
I then added some more minor features while hitting my peak popularity online with this client.
I kept on adding things until I finally felt I was with version 5 being the last version of this client. In fact, it was not just the last version of this client, but the last time I ever attempted this form of unethical cheating in general.
But while this was the last form of a modded client I ever indulged in, this was just the start of my coding journey. Including creating my own plugins, maintaining a server, and in the end studying software engineering.
